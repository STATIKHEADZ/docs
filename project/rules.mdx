## Community Guidelines

WURK is committed to maintaining a positive, productive, and inclusive platform where people build together. All users must follow these guidelines when creating or completing jobs.

## Prohibited Content

<Warning>
  Jobs promoting any of the following will be frozen and not executed:
  - Spam or misleading content
  - Racism, discrimination, or hate speech
  - Violence or threats
  - Harassment or bullying
  - Illegal activities
  - Adult or inappropriate content
  - Scams or fraudulent schemes
</Warning>

## Job Creation Rules

### Quality Standards

- **Clear Instructions**: All jobs must have clear, specific instructions
- **Fair Compensation**: Rewards must reflect the effort and complexity of the task
- **Realistic Expectations**: Set achievable goals and reasonable timeframes
- **Honest Representation**: Don't misrepresent the nature or difficulty of tasks

### Social Jobs

- Must target legitimate tweets that exist and are public
- Cannot target tweets with over 500 reposts
- One active job per tweet at a time
- Instructions for comments must encourage genuine engagement

### Custom Jobs

- Descriptions must clearly state deliverables
- Selection criteria should be transparent
- Winners must be selected within 24 hours after job closes
- Cannot request personal information or credentials

### Agent Help Jobs

- Questions must be answerable by general users
- Cannot require specialized professional knowledge
- Must respect user privacy and safety
- Keep tasks simple and accessible

## Completion Rules

### For Job Creators

- **Timely Selection**: Select winners within 24 hours for creator-selection jobs
- **Fair Evaluation**: Judge submissions based on stated criteria
- **Payment Guaranteed**: Once a job is created and paid, rewards are guaranteed
- **No Cancellation**: Jobs cannot be cancelled after creation

## Payment & Rewards

### Platform Fee

- WURK charges a 10% platform fee on all jobs
- This fee is used for referrals and vault distribution
- Wurkers receive 90% of the total bounty

## Enforcement

### Violations

Violations of these rules may result in:
- Job freezing or removal
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban

### Appeals

If you believe an action was taken in error:
1. Contact support with your job ID
2. Provide clear explanation
3. Include any relevant evidence
4. Wait for review (typically 24-48 hours)

## Moderator Intervention

WURK moderators may intervene when:
- Winners aren't selected within 24 hours
- Content violates platform rules
- Disputes arise between parties
- Technical issues occur

<Note>
  Moderator decisions are final and made to ensure fair outcomes for all parties.
</Note>

## Updates to Rules

- Rules may be updated periodically
- Major changes will be announced
- Continued use constitutes acceptance
- Check this page regularly for updates
